About Us

We’re Setting the Standards in Research & Clinical Care

QuickMed was created by LordGhostX, Ochuko & Destiny to solve problems associated with costs & running time in medical tests and diagnostics. Many diagnostic issues are data-driven and resolving them requires new data-science based approaches. QuickMed is focused on using innovative AI and machine-learning techniques to improve diagnostic accuracy, cut costs, decrease testing time and improve patient safety worldwide.

Our Product

QuickMed has the ability to perform a variety of diagnostics autonomously with artificial intelligence including but not limited to the following...

Malaria Cell Detection

QuickMed web platform has the ability to detect malaria cells in their early stages so the patient can start taking early treatment leading to lesser medical costs. 

Skin Cancer Classification

QuickMed also has the feature to classify skin cancer as either malignant or benign with only an image of the skin which will eventually save up cost of taking the test with the same accuracy levels as actually taking it.

Thorax Disease Identification

QuickMed also has the ability to detect up to 15 disease patterns of thorax disease from a given X-ray image sample. This will help hospitals automate radiologist analysis saving costs and also getting better accuracy scores.

Optical Coherence Tomography Analysis

QuickMed platfrom can perform retinal optical coherence tomography analysis in realtime. This type of analysis typically takes about 10 minutes and equipment for such tests cost up to $35K to $150K.

Our Approach with AI

Autonomous

Our AI systems are autonomous and assistive - enabling disease detection in primary care that would typically involve a specialist or special equipment.

Explanative

Our deep learning-based detectors search for the same signs of disease as a clinician, so we know how the AI came to its decision. It is able to surpass specialists in result accuracy, delivery time & cost.

Clinically Based

Our algorithms are developed with insights from leading medical experts and are based on existing clinical practice guidelines.

Lab-quality results without a lab

QuickMed is playing a part in the future of medicine by applying artificial intelligence (AI) to diagnostics – making tests more automated, more accurate and therefore safer.

Embedding AI into diagnostics removes opportunities for human error and saves clinical labs time and money. For medical professionals, AI-enabled diagnostics result in more suitable patient treatment plans. For patients, diagnostics that are more reliable increase safety and limit preventable deaths.

At a global level, real-time data acquisition and AI-based standardisation has the potential to allow governments and NGOs to more effectively manage resources using data-driven monitoring of infection spread. By reducing need for trained staff, AI also helps resolve globally recognised staff-shortage issues.
